Museum Name:  Heritage of India Museum  
 
Location:   
1234, Maharaja Street, Old City, Jaipur, Rajasthan, India  
 
Overview:  
The Heritage of India Museum showcases the rich cultural and historical legacy of India. It 
features a vast collection of artifacts, sculptures, paintings, textiles, and ancient manuscripts 
that reflect India's diverse traditions and artistic heritage. The museum has specialized sections 
dedicated to different states, ancient civilizations, and historical events, making it a must -visit 
for history enthusiasts and tourists alike.
